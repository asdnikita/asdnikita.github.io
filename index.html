Rideoo Extreme Scootering Event

DESCRIPTION

Rideoo Extreme Scootering Tour is an yearly event that gathers most passionate scooter riders around the globe to have unforgettable 14 day trip around the Europe's most fanmous skateparks. This trip is designed to popularize active lifestyle and to bring some inspiration to the growing generations.

The trip will start in Berlin (Germany) where all applicants will embark to the specially designed Rideoo Scooter Van. Each few days the Van will be stopping in the famous skateparks to have fun and meet new people there.
